AI automation & growth systems for US companies

Frequently asked questions

What does TechGen Labs do?

TechGen Labs is a US digital product studio that builds AI agents, workflow automation, custom software, web & mobile apps, branding and growth systems for companies with roughly 50–500 employees.

Who does TechGen Labs work with?

Founders, COOs, operations and RevOps leaders at US SMB and mid-market companies (about 50–500 employees) in B2B services, e-commerce and healthcare-adjacent operations.

How much does an AI agent cost?

Simple pilots run $5K–$50K, mid-tier builds $20K–$80K, and advanced multi-agent systems $100K+. We scope a fixed-price pilot up front — see the full 2026 AI agent pricing guide.

How fast can you ship a project?

Most builds ship a working demo within 2–3 weeks and a fixed-scope pilot in 4–6 weeks, with a working demo every Friday so you see progress before committing further.

What makes TechGen Labs different?

One senior team across four practices — AI & automation, design, development and growth — with no handoffs, fixed-scope pilots, and a working demo every Friday.
